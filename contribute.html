How to contribute to this generator

  1. Make the template. Here's a few examples to get you started:

    2. The templates must be:

    • Grayscale
    • 64x64 (no HD skins)
    • Eyes, Hair, Skin, or Clothes (more options coming soon).

  2. Are there any pixels you don't want to be tinted? (like the whites of an eye)
    • If so, you'll need to make a mask layer.
    • Mask layers tell the program which pixels to tint.
    • To make a mask layer, split your image into two files: One image for all pixels you don't want tinted, and one for all pixels you do want tinted.
  3. Send the image(s) to me via a skin sharing website (like Planet Minecraft), file sharing site (Mediafire, Dropbox), or even Github.
